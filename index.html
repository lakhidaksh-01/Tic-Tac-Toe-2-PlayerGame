How to Play

  1. Player X always starts first. The active player's turn is indicated by the red highlight.
  2. Click on any empty box to make your move. The game alternates between Player X and Player O.
  3. To start a new game, click the Reset Game button.
  4. The game ends when a player gets three marks in a row, column, or diagonal, or when all boxes are filled (resulting in a tie).
